Who We Are

We are a fashion forward company that caters for corporate, social bespoke and everyday wear.

The company has been around for several years with our Head Designer and founder Boniswa Mngomezulu who has gained experience from the likes of Quiteria & George with vast knowledge and expertise that has given her an edge to create beautiful, inspired pieces for everyday people who want to use fashion as an expression.

Define

We are interested in fashion and creating gem pieces that are fashionably on trend. We experiment with new and innovative styles while creating new looks. We disrupt status quos as we create outstanding fashion for fearless individuals who want to stand out.

Design

Our designs are curated and crafted to last, made with soft textures and materials that are breathable and elegant. A combination of modern and edgy fashionable wear curated to stand the test of times.

Fashion

Our creations are fit for kings and queens, better yet for Gods and Goddesses. We pride our selves with our fashion forward edgy wear with inspirations that’s over 200 years old history of quintessential boho fashion with a touch of street style for bold yet modest beings that are willing use fashion as an extended expression.
